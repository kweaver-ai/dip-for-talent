# KWeaver Intelligent Talent Analytics Solution (Brief)

English | [中文](README.zh.md)

KWeaver targets mid-to-large organizations in HR and business management. It emphasizes trusted data, reliable logic, and explainable decisions. By integrating multi-source talent and business data, it delivers a unified analytics and decision-support capability, turning complex, fragmented talent issues into actionable management decisions.

## Solution Overview (Design Doc Summary)
- **Core modules**: Talent Insight Workbench, Human Capital ROI Analysis, Role-People Dynamic Matching, Organization Health Analysis, Core Talent Incentive Effectiveness Analysis, Talent Attrition Risk Analysis, plus Action Center, Data Center, and Configuration Center.
- **ROI baseline**: ROI measures efficiency between output gains and input costs and serves as a key management metric.

## ROI Analysis (roi-analysis Design Doc Summary)
- **Business goals**: Quantify the relationship between people inputs and business outcomes, support org-level drill-down, and inform org adjustments, incentive strategy, and resource reallocation.
- **Typical scenarios**: Monthly/quarterly performance reviews, region/department comparisons, pre-adjustment evaluation, and quantified decisions for incentive or headcount changes.
- **Key functions**:
  - ROI KPI overview: cards show current value, benchmark, attainment rate, and YoY trend with drill-down into definitions and trends.
  - Org ROI distribution: org tree view of ROI performance by level with comparison and drill-down.
  - Efficiency-related factor analysis: indicators strongly correlated with ROI, correlation coefficients, directionality, and impact interpretation.
  - Copilot interaction: explanations and Q&A on ROI metrics, trends, root causes, and action suggestions.
